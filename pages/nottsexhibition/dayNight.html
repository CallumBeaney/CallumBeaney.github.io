I still have these frames and folded scrolls, as well as the big reusable wallpaper prints. I would really like to show this work again some time, probably titled `Day & Night`. The night time work I named `No Flashlight` after the Mount Eerie album of the same name. I still have a few of the zines knocking around and will gladly send you one if you contact me.

I put this exhibition together in Autumn 2018, after I won a printing bursary with Genesis Imaging. Most of these installs are by Mike Kane.
