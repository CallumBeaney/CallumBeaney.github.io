This was a follow-up show to Single Surface that my collaborator Eugenie Shinkle headed with the gallery project Lake (Nishant Shukla & James Wilkie) in Deptford, London. Speakers above the metal sheets played ambient sound made James, created by converting the visual information in the book into sound signatures. I shot all the original prints. The framed pieces are Eugenie's, as is the wall piece. Nishant curated the space. As I was unable to participate in this exhibition I mostly offered suggestions and feedback from afar. On the closing night Folium (Stewart Hardie & Harry Gammer-Flitcroft) played an improvised ambient/noise work in on the work using field recordings, contact mics, and tape loops.
