關於我頁面

They always face away, never quite focusing on the lens itself.

There is something out there, another world inhabited by these euphoric people, filled with every joy they could possibly desire—possibly need—frozen in place before us. Their bared teeth and curled lip resembling a corpse’s rictus grin or the warning snarl our primate ancestors display when threatened, they share with us their ecstasy. Despite our technological procession, there is a primitive instinct to identify with them, to believe in their promise—ghosts resurrected by our conviction that, through them, we too can attain this kind of happiness, this unending bliss.

They are—they will be—happy forever — glowing forever.
