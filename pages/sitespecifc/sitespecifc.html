This book was published by Tall Poppy Press, co-authored with Matt Dunne, and designed by Rebecca McCauley.

Over the years —but especially over COVID— many photographic artist, gallery and museum websites have gone down, and with them often also a rich history of hard work & creativity. Matt & I found a want for a more physical record of some of the great work people have done over the last 10-15 years in photography able to function as a reference book for people looking for inspiration. Together we researched & reached out to photographers.
　　`Site Specific` features 60 artists from around the world, including: Aleix Plademunt, Geraldine Kang, Anne Erhard, Trent Parke, Luis Antonio Santos, Hanqing & Mona, Noah Kalina, Arif Furqan, Hiroko Komatsu, and Dayanita Singh.
