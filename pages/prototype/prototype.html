The Prototype is a project Eugenie Shinkle & I made together from a set of photographs I took of wooden blocks and their shadows. We were inspired a bit by Futurist painting and the chaotic and abrasive sounds of noise/industrial music, but we were mostly experimenting with images in terms of rhythms and shapes, patterns and textures. A version of it was published by Folium Publishing in 2022. The book is a mix of some of the book dummies we made together. The first ever dummy was the one at the bottom.

This was the book launch event, we also put prints and a video up and played Ink Spots and made friends.

This was the original zine I made. I'd like to make more but need a good printer and a similiar paper.
