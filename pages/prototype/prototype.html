The Prototype is a combination of photocopied book dummies Eugenie Shinkle & I made together from the same set of photographs of wooden blocks and their shadows, playing with rhythms and shapes, patterns and textures. It is an experiment in making images that feel like the chaotic and abrasive sounds of noise & industrial music, one iteration of an ongoing project with no defined outcome. It was released by Folium Publishing in 2022.
