At the Digital Content Unit, Cambridge University Library, I photographed historic manuscripts, scrolls, and maps using Phase One, Capture One, Adobe Photoshop, Profoto lighting, Golden Thread and custom scanning equipment according to ISO standards, working on:

  • Austrian dramatist Arthur Schnitzler's notes, diaries and manuscripts for publication in Arthur Schnitzler digital: Digital Critical Edition.
  • Over 100 large maps using the DCU's custom XYZ Table, stitching images together using PTGui.
  • A collection of geneticist William Bateson's journals.
  • Assorted small collections of books, photographs and magazines, an archive of historical golf magazines, colonial-era documentation of India, and photographic negative film.
